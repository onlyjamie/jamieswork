ABOUT GAMEFACE

Gameface was borne out of a desire to fix a broken model and ended up creating a new industry. Headquartered in Boston amongst similarly game-changing startups in Downtown Crossing, the Gameface Media team has backgrounds in finance, technology, sports, photography, and more. The majority of us are active athletes in Gameface events, but no worries – we love having cheerleaders, too.
