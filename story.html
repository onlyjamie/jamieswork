OUR STORY

In 2013, active amateur athletes David Lavallee & Bob Crowley decided the then-current model of amateur sports photography – which lacked accessibility, quality, and value – was overdue for a makeover. Their vision of sports photography focused on capturing high quality photos of these memorable moments and then making them available in days, easy to download and share from any device, and most importantly: free.

In just 2 years, Gameface Media has become the leading publisher of free amateur sports photos in the world. This year alone we’ve shot over 10 million photos of 2 million athletes across 600+ events, and released our legacy collection of photos from the 1950s on.

We’re champions of the everyday athlete. And we’re hiring!
